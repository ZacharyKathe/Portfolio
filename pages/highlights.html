Property Ownership Site

I created The Property Ownership Site for the City of Lake Stevens and it helps the public determine property ownership Between the County and City of Lake Stevens. I get the Snohomish County data from their GIS API and the City data is coming from an Esri Enterprise on-premise solution.
The City Park Locations Map was a fun build and gives city residents an easy way to find a park to play at!
